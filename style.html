Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto's dream is to become Hokage!

Naruto is a young ninja who strives to be recognized by his peers and to fulfill his dream of becoming the leader of his village, the Hokage. Along the way, he faces many challenges and makes loyal friends.

"I'm not gonna run away, I never go back on my word! That's my nindo: my ninja way!" - Naruto Uzumaki
